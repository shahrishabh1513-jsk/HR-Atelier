Our Story

Elevating Style Since 2026

HR FashionStyle was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to make premium fashion accessible to everyone who appreciates quality and style. What started as a small boutique in Vadodara has grown into a trusted destination for fashion enthusiasts across India.

Our journey began when our founder, Harshil Rathod, noticed a gap in the market for affordable luxury that didn't compromise on quality. With a passion for fashion and an eye for detail, he set out to curate collections that blend contemporary trends with timeless elegance.

Today, HR FashionStyle stands as a testament to the belief that everyone deserves to look and feel their best. We've helped thousands of customers discover their unique style and express themselves through fashion.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

From humble beginnings to your favorite fashion destination

2019

The Beginning

HR FashionStyle started as a small pop-up store in Alkapuri, Vadodara, with just 50 products and a dream.

2020

First Anniversary & Digital Launch

Celebrated our first year and launched our online store, reaching customers beyond Vadodara for the first time.

2021

Expansion & New Collections

Expanded our product range to include men's fashion and accessories. Crossed 10,000 happy customers.

2022

Sustainability Initiative

Launched eco-friendly packaging and partnered with sustainable brands. Received "Emerging Fashion Brand" award.

2023

Physical Flagship Store

Opened our first flagship store in Vadodara, creating an immersive fashion destination for our community.

2024

National Recognition

Featured in "Top 10 Emerging Fashion Brands" by Fashion India. Expanded shipping to over 500 cities.

2025

Community Milestone

Reached 50,000+ happy customers and 1000+ products. Launched our loyalty program and community initiatives.

2026

Today & Beyond

Continuing our journey to bring you the best in fashion, with exciting new collections and experiences on the horizon.
